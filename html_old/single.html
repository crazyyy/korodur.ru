Heavy-duty hard aggregate fast screed

Nowadays enormous time pressure rules the job site routine. The demand for fast repairs of destructed industrial floors is growing. Long drying times of customary cement screeds present a problem in view of the fixed time window for the application. KORODUR offers a time saving solution, primarily for industrial floors.

The innovative product NEODUR HE 50 rapid combines perfectly the properties “fast setting” and “highly wear resistant” – a fast screed for the durably stressed industrial floor. Ready for stress already after 48 h!

Depending the demanded stressability, the dry mortar NEODUR HE 50 rapid is supplied in the requested quality. According to KORODUR‘s core competency, the products NEODUR HE 50 rapid are factory-produced with hard aggregates of coating groups A, M and KS acc. to DIN 1100.

The system NEODUR rapid is applied in composite on set base concrete in layer thickness of 10 mm to max. 30 mm.
